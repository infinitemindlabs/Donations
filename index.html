The Story Behind EasyReader

The Struggle

Reading has always been a challenge for me. I would often miss lines or lose my place, struggle to remember what was written on the page before, and sometimes I'd even get nauseous from my eyes darting left to right over and over, page after page.

The Solution

EasyReader, a new way to read. With the help of EasyReader, I've been able to tackle reading more confidently than ever before. It has become an essential tool in my quest to learn more quickly and I encourage you to share it with people who struggle with reading too.

- Mike from InfiniteMindLabs
